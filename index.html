James Oliver Rigney Jr: Novelist Extrodinaire

Also known by his famous pseudonym, Robert Jordan stands against a cinder block wall wearing a grey wool trench coat and black hat
  • Born October 17, 1948 to James Oliver Rigny and Eva May Grooms in Charleston, SC.
  • From 1968-70 he served two tours in Vietnam, earning the Distinguished Service Cross, the Bronze Star, and two Vietnamese Gallentry Crosses.
  • Graduated from The Citadel with a BS in physics in 1974
  • In 1978 he quit his career as a nuclear engineer to pursue writing full time because "life is too short."
  • March 28, 1981 James marries Harriet Mcdougal (who also becomes his editor) giving him a son.
  • Passed away September 17, 2007 after a tenuous battle with cardiac amyloidosis
